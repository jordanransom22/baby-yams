I am in my last semster at the University of North Texas and will be graduating in December with a B.A in English and a minor in Technical Writing. I have been composing creative writing pieces for 8 years now, and my works range anywhere from short stories to novels to even poetry. I have a poem published in a small library in Houston, TX and I hope to get more intricate and lengthy pieces published in the future! My interests are reading and listening to music on my own, and playing video games with my husband Kyle. Feel free to take a look around my page and get to know me and my writing style a little more!
